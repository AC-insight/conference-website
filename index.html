Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To bridge the gap between scientific discovery and practical application, making traditional physical activities more accessible, measurable, and sustainable through technology integration.

Vision

To establish a new paradigm for exercise-based healthy aging by 2035, guided by the collective expertise of Asia's leading researchers.

Founding Chair

Shuzo Kumagai, Ph.D.

The visionary founder of the AC-INSIGHT Consortium.
Leading the transformation of geriatric healthcare through innovative research.
