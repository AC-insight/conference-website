Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To bridge the gap between scientific discovery and practical application, making traditional physical activities more accessible, measurable, and sustainable through technology integration.

Vision

To establish a new paradigm for exercise-based healthy aging by 2035, guided by the collective expertise of Asia's leading researchers.

Core Research Domains

Traditional Exercise Interventions

Systematically evaluating and optimizing conventional exercise programs.

E-sports for Training

Creating revolutionary platforms at the intersection of gaming technology and gerontology.

Digital Health Translation

Developing comprehensive ecosystems that seamlessly blend traditional exercise with modern tech.

Sustainable Strategies

Ensuring all innovations translate into sustainable real-world programs.
